How the Super Hybrid System Works

01

Intelligent Power Management

The system intelligently switches between electric and gasoline power based on driving conditions, optimizing efficiency and performance in real-time.

02

Regenerative Braking

Energy typically lost during braking is captured and converted back into electricity, continuously recharging the battery system.

03

Seamless Integration

The transition between electric and gasoline power is completely seamless, providing a smooth and refined driving experience.

04

Advanced Battery Technology

High-capacity lithium-ion batteries provide reliable power storage with exceptional longevity and fast charging capabilities.
